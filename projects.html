Img.Share

Img.Share is a media sharing bot where you can post and get random images!


Commands: (may be outdated)


Get

allmemes: Shows all of the memes

allpets: Shows all of the pets

certainmeme: Gets a certain meme

certainpet: Gets a certain pet

randomeme: Gets a random meme

randompet: Gets a random pet


Post

postmeme: Posts A Meme to the Meme folder

postpet: Posts A Pet to the pet folder


Other

guidelines: Shows the Guidelines

invite: Sends an invite to the bot

ping: Pings the bot

reload: Reloads all/one of the bots cogs

server: Sends an invite to join the official Img.Share server


Guidelines:

No NSFW

No Deepfakes (looking at you oscie)

No Racisim

If you wouldnt show your friends/family it, dont post it (eg: dont post anything offensive)
